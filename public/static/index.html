Use your real location

If you use a fake location your account may be blocked by Pokemon GO, especially if you hop multiple locations.

Get a Google Maps Key

You need to register for a Get a Google Maps Token or you will get errors that the you are past the daily limits.

  • Click 'Get A Key' (upper right)
  • Click 'Continue'
  • Click 'Continue' to Create a Project
  • Name your project whatever you want (such as Pokemap)
  • Copy the Key (starts with 'AI')
  • Put the key in node-pokemap/PokemonGo-Map-web-client/static/config.js





Only sign in with one account at a time

You cannot use Pokemon GO and Pokemap with the same account at the same time or you may be blocked by the Pokemon GO.

Use your Pokemon Trainer Account

Register on Pokemon.com for a Pokemon Trainer Account. You can use your Google Account, but using a PTC may be more private.
